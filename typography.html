Typography

Sen

We chose sans-serif Sen as our main header font for its simple yet contemporary and elegant look.
Sen complements our body font (Hind) and adds a touch of friendliness and playfulness with is geometric, rounded design.
Sen comes in regular, bold and extra bold. Sen is compatible with 465 languages in the world.

Sen Bold

Hind

Hind is a versatile and modern sans-serif font, making it a great choice for our body font. It has a clean and simple design for legibility and it supports the overall look of our app. Our app has a lot of textual information, this slightly condensed font helps maximize space efficiency without sacrificing readability. Hind comes with 4 variations: regular, medium, semi-bold and bold. Hind is compatible with 233 other languages.

ZT Gatha

The "ZT Gatha" typeface is only utilized in our logo. It blends seamlessly with "Sen," our header font, particularly as we integrate the logo with app's name. We consistently employ the semi-bold weight for this font.
