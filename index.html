Logo Rationale

While experimenting with logo ideas, we aimed to find balance in simplicity and storytelling. While attaching a logo to our companies vision was difficult we cycled through options and ended up choosing our current design for multiple reasons. Our app prioritizes simplicity and community at its core, while working our our logo this was our foremost thought. The Neighbourly logo is a simple design of a N with rounded edges which portrays an inviting feeling to community members. The logo is shaped as a house to represent our vision of connecting people with services in their neighbourhood. The colour scheme of myrtle green, white, and vanilla was chosen to match the apps general theme while portraying an inviting family based image with modern colours. The dark myrtle green contrasts well with both vanilla yellow and white which allows the logo to stand out using any of the three colour combinations. This contrasts allows the logo to be scaled easily and is readable for users at almost all sizes. Spacing the decorative aspects of the logo with the letter N was included but minimized to allow the user to associate the two graphics together while simultaneously allowing the eye to still read out the company name Neighbourly.
