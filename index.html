Hello, We Are Neighbourly!

Neighbourly is a community driven app that lets everyone helps everyone! Within Neighbourly you can help others with tasks, or do the tasks to help others. Making some extra money and helping your community at the same time. Additionally, you can attend classes held by our mentors or partners, and learn a variety of new skills!

Neighbourly is designed & developed by Holden Budiman, Jasmine Putri and Jackson Saini. While we're still in development, feel free to get to know us a little bit more!
